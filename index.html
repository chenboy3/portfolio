DashboardMD

DashboardMD is a web-app that makes surgeon transcripts immediately available to deaf residents post-surgery.

With DashboardMD, a surgeon is able to open up the iOS app and start talking while going over the surgery live, and can then submit the surgery for transmission, where the speech gets transcribed into a text format, allowing deaf surgeons/residents to view the transcript in real time alongside the vitals during the surgery itself.

I worked on the front-end of this webapp, adding React features to animate the transcription and sync it to the graph of the vitals, so that the vitals and transcription are presented in live-time, allowing audience members to following along the transcription as if replaying the surgery live.

Technical Skills: Python, Flask, Azure, iOS Swift, React

APIs: Revature
